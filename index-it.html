The Energy of the Three Valleys

Since time immemorial, nature has been a source of strength, vitality and endurance. In the heart of the Iberian Peninsula, three regions came together to give birth to a unique drink, the perfect result of the fusion of flavour and natural power.

The Power of the Velilla Blueberry

In Velilla del Río Carrión, where crystal-clear rivers flow through the mountains, deep blue blueberries grow, reflecting their purity and richness in antioxidants. Since ancient times, villagers have relied on this fruit to keep their energy up on the demanding mountain roads.

The Power of the Cherry of the Sierra de France

In the Sierra de Francia, Salamanca, the cherry trees blossom a vibrant red, a symbol of vitality. For generations, these fruits have been known for their ability to revitalise the body, providing freshness and stamina in a natural way.

The Resistance of Bierzo Honey

From the hills of the Bierzo, golden honey has been a treasure trove of natural energy. Used for centuries as a source of vigour and recuperation, its sweetness not only enhances the flavour, but also provides the boost needed to keep going.

A drink that unites nature and energy

Inspired by the power of these three ingredients, our drink combines the best of nature to hydrate and revitalise. Its unique formula offers a perfect balance of freshness, taste and functionality, ideal for those looking to push their limits, stay active and face every challenge with renewed energy.

Discover the essence of the three valleys. Recharge your body, awaken your energy and conquer your day.
